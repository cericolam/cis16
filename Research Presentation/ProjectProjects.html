Project Projects is a New York based design firm. It was founded in 2004 by its currents principals, Prem Krishnamurthy and Adam Michaels. Their work focuses on the cultural sector, with clients ranging from architects to artists and educators.

They work both digitally and in print, their projects spanning many mediums. They have been involved in brand identity, exhibition design, books, magazines, signage, and web design.

The involvement they have in their projects often goes beyond design, expanding into ongoing curatorial and communication consulting, as well as press support.
